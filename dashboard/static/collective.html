The Collective

Each ktrader instance makes independent predictions. The collective aggregates them anonymously. Every member's bot gets access to the crowd signal -- average probability, consensus direction, confidence distribution -- without exposing identity, P&L, or positions.

Why the cap? The edge is inversely proportional to the number of participants. With hundreds of members, each gets meaningful alpha. At mass adoption, the collective signal converges to the market price itself. The cap keeps the collective useful.
How it works
  1. Your bot analyzes a market and picks a side.
  2. It sends an anonymized signal: probability, direction, confidence. Nothing else.
  3. The server aggregates and returns the crowd view for that market.
  4. Your bot folds crowd data into its next decision. Better information, same autonomy.
